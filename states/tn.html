State Profile

Tamil Nadu

Capital: Chennai

Tamil Nadu is home to massive stone Dravidian temples, classical Bharatanatyam dance, Carnatic music, and a history stretching back to the ancient Sangam age.\n\nThe vibrant region of Tamil Nadu is governed from its beautiful capital, Chennai. The land is deeply connected to its roots, celebrating grand occasions like Pongal & Puthandu with unmatched energy and devotion.\n\nVisitors from around the world are mesmerized by the local culinary delights, particularly the iconic Dosa, Idli-Sambar & Pongal, which represents centuries of refined regional cooking techniques and traditional spices. A journey to Tamil Nadu is a journey through time, culture, and nature.

Cultural Highlights

📍Capital: Chennai
🍛Famous Food: Dosa, Idli-Sambar & Pongal
🎉Major Festival: Pongal & Puthandu
