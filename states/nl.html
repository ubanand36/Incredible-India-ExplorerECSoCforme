State Profile

Nagaland

Capital: Kohima

Nagaland is a vibrant mountainous state famous for its rich tribal warrior heritage, beautiful woven shawls, and the Hornbill Festival.\n\nThe vibrant region of Nagaland is governed from its beautiful capital, Kohima. The land is deeply connected to its roots, celebrating grand occasions like Hornbill Festival with unmatched energy and devotion.\n\nVisitors from around the world are mesmerized by the local culinary delights, particularly the iconic Smoked Pork with Bamboo Shoot, which represents centuries of refined regional cooking techniques and traditional spices. A journey to Nagaland is a journey through time, culture, and nature.

Cultural Highlights
